unud buktun tuw-ow (3 heads) niya triceps muscle (3 heads). Located at the upper arm (posterior), opposite the biceps at the front. Its main function is to extend the elbow joint so the arm straightens away from the body. Heads: Long head, Lateral head, Medial head.

unud duwa-ow (2 heads) kabaakan ha unahan sin buktun ha taas biceps muscle (2 heads). Located at the anterior upper arm. Its function is to flex or pull the forearm toward the body.
